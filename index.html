Counter to Goal App

This is an efficiency app for people that trying to achieve ambitious goals.

Counter to Goal:
When you have a goal, you have a specific date. However, you need to see how many days left to the date. In this time, Counter to Goal comes great advantage. Set an amazing goal then see how many days passed or left!
